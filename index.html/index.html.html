Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What types of projects do you specialize in?

We focus exclusively on premium health, fitness, beauty and personal care brands. Our expertise includes website copy, product descriptions, email sequences, sales pages, and ad campaigns. We work with established brands looking to elevate their messaging as well as startups needing foundational brand voice development.

What's your typical process for a new client?

We begin with a comprehensive discovery phase where We learn about your brand, audience, and goals. Then We conduct market research and develop a content strategy. After your approval, We craft the copy and we refine through 1-2 revision rounds. Finally, We provide conversion optimization recommendations based on performance data.

How do you measure the success of your copy?

We track key metrics including conversion rates, engagement, time on page, and ROI. For each project, we establish clear KPIs upfront. We provide detailed performance reports and continue to optimize based on data. Most clients see a 30-200% increase in conversions within the first 60 days.

What's your availability and lead time?

We maintain limited availability to ensure each client receives premium attention. Current lead time for new projects is 5-7 days. Rush projects may be accommodated based on availability with a 25% expedite fee. We recommend reaching out at least 2 weeks before your ideal launch date.
